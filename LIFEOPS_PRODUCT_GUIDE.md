**"Is it hard to use?"**  
No. Connect device, wait a week, start asking questions.

**"Will I have to do a lot?"**  
No. It's automatic. You can log manually if you want, but you don't have to.

**"What if I miss days?"**  
Totally fine. It works with whatever data it has.

**"Is my data private?"**  
Yes. Stored on your device. You control everything.

The base system is free. Optional AI features use your API keys.

**"Can I turn it off?"**  
Anytime. Your data stays unless you delete it.

Fitbit, Apple Watch, smart cameras, or manual entry.

**"How long until I see results?"**  
Simple insights in 3 days. Deep patterns in 2-4 weeks.
